Frequently Asked Questions

Our standard lease agreements are for 12 months, but we also offer 6-month and month-to-month options for some properties. Please inquire about specific properties for available lease terms.
Utility inclusion varies by property. Some of our rentals include water and trash services, while others require tenants to set up all utilities. The property listings specify which utilities, if any, are included in the monthly rent.
To apply for one of our rental properties, you'll need to complete an application form, provide proof of income (typically 3x the monthly rent), consent to a credit and background check, and pay an application fee. We also require references from previous landlords.
